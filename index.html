F.A.Q

Want to learn more?.

We believe clarity is the first step to a great partnership. Find quick answers about our process and promises below. Your question not covered? Let's talk.

What is your typical project timeline?
For a Minimum Viable Product (MVP), timelines typically range from 3-6 months. The final timeline depends on the project's complexity, which we will define together during the Discovery phase. We work in agile sprints, so you'll see a working product early and often.
What does the collaboration process look like?
We believe in total transparency. You'll have a dedicated Project Lead, weekly sprint review demos, and access to a project management dashboard. We use Slack, Zoom, and Jira to keep communication fluid and constant.
How do you handle project pricing?
We offer flexible models to suit your needs: Fixed Price for well-defined projects, Time & Materials for agile, evolving projects, and Dedicated Team for long-term partnerships. We'll recommend the best model for you after our initial discussion.
